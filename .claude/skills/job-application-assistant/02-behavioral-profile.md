# Behavioral Profile

## Overview
Pushpak Roy's available resume evidence indicates a structured, detail-oriented, controls-minded work style. He is strongest in roles that require accurate records, careful review, data validation, discrepancy investigation, and coordination with stakeholders to resolve issues.

## Core Behavioral Drives

| Drive | Level | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Accuracy and reliability | High | Pushpak's work centers on keeping transactions, records, and reports complete, supported, and audit-ready. |
| Analytical follow-through | High | He compares data sources, investigates variances, documents corrections, and follows issues through remediation. |
| Structure and process | High | He works well where approvals, documentation, system records, and standardized capture rules matter. |
| Stakeholder communication | Moderate to high | He coordinates with cross-functional stakeholders to resolve missing documents, pricing discrepancies, and data issues. |

## Strongest Behaviors
- **Control orientation:** Reviews approvals, authorizations, and supporting documents before transactions enter accounting records.
- **Data quality mindset:** Validates enterprise system records and preserves audit trails through documented corrections.
- **Practical problem solving:** Uses reconciliations, Excel analysis, and stakeholder follow-up to identify and resolve discrepancies.

## How You Work Best

- On tasks where accuracy, completeness, and traceability are valued.
- With teams that need a bridge between accounting records, enterprise systems, and operational stakeholders.
